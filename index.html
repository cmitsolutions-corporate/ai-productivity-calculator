Methodology and sources

This is a planning estimate, not a guarantee of cash savings or revenue.

Productivity benchmark: A large field study of customer-support agents found that access to a generative AI assistant improved productivity by about 15% on average.

Compensation method: The calculator converts annual salary into a loaded hourly cost by adding the selected benefits and payroll overhead percentage, then dividing by annual working hours.
