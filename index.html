How the estimate works

The calculator converts annual salary into an hourly employee cost using 2,080 work hours, then adds the selected benefits and payroll overhead.

Eligible employees × adoption × AI-suitable hours × productivity gain × productive reuse × working weeks × calculated hourly employee cost − AI costs
This is a planning estimate, not a guarantee. Results depend on adoption, training, workflow design, data quality, governance, and whether saved time is redirected to productive work.

Benchmarks used

15% productivity starting point: A field study of 5,172 customer-support agents found an average 15% improvement with generative AI assistance.

30% overhead estimate: A practical default for benefits, payroll taxes, insurance, and related employment costs. Users can change or remove it.
